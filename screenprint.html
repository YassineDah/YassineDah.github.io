ScreenPrinting Project

What I Did

For this project, I made a screen print. I designed it on Photoshop first, then transferred it onto a screen and printed it.

How I Did It

1. Made the Design

I used Photoshop to create my design with text and graphics.

2. Built the Frame

I cut wood at a 45° angle and stapled it together to hold the mesh.

3. Made the Stencil

I cut my design into vinyl, put it on the mesh, and heated it so it stuck. This made the edges sharp.

4. Printed It

I put the mesh over the paper/fabric, pulled ink across with a squeegee, and the design transferred onto it.

5. Let It Dry

I let the ink dry and checked the print to make sure it looked good.

Materials I Used

  • Photoshop
  • Wood frame
  • Mesh screen
  • Vinyl
  • Heat source
  • Ink
  • Squeegee
  • Paper or fabric

Challenges

The hardest parts were thinking of a design to make and actually building the wood frame. Cutting the wood at the right angle and getting it to stay together was tricky.
